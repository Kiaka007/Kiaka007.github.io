Exhibition // Because this is My First Life

As children we are taught to see failures as learning opportunities. “Next time” we say, “Next time I’ll know to do this or to say that.” We hope that as we grow each adversity can be met with more intention, more skill, and hopefully more grace.

In “Because This is My First Life” I explore feelings left behind by events that only happen once in our lives. Events that we cannot walk away from and say “Next time…”; events that we are forced to face as we are to the best of our abilities.
