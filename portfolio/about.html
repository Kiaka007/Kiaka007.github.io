About

Kyoko Eng is a Nashville based experiential artist and designer. Her digital spaces, performances, and mixed-media artifacts examine the underlying tensions between Nature & Technology, Privacy & AI, and the perception of the Self vs. the Collective. She currently serves as the UX/UI lead for OpenMined’s opensource library PySyft. Her recent work explores how interfaces can help lower the barrier to using Privacy Enhancing Technologies (PETs).

Some notable projects in this space have been Christ Church Call Initiative on Algorithmic Outcomes (2023), The U.S. Census and UN PET Lab (2022), and Twitter’s Advancing Algorithmic Transparency (2022). She is a board member of the Nashville Dance Collective and is a 2023-2024 Artwire Fellow at Oz Arts. She holds a B.F.A. in Graphic Design from the University of Tennessee Knoxville.
