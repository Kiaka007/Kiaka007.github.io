Venue Details

The venue entrance can be a little tricky to find. As you go down Pulaski Hwy you will see the First Baptist Church of Columbia on the right and to the left will be a small road that looks like a driveway with signage for our wedding. You will take a left onto that small road and follow it down a ways. At the end of that road will be the venue.

Parking will be available at the venue. When you face the house take a left and a winding road will take you to the back of the house were a field has been marked for parking. There will be signage to help guide.

Attire

The wedding and reception will be informal. Think "garden party". You can be as casual as you would like. The Ceremony & Reception will be outside if the weather permits, with that being the case we recommend bringing a jacket or cardigan as the reception moves into the evening. For those wanting to wear heels, lawn games & dancing will be available so you might want to bring flats or sandals to change into or you can take off the heels and go barefoot like I plan to ^_^

What if I can’t make it?

If you cannot make it don’t worry. Just let us know as soon as you are able so that we can plan accordingly.

Are kids welcome?

Yes we would love to see your kiddos there, please make a note in the RSVP form if your kids are coming so that we can accommodate for seating.

Can I bring a date?

If you do not have a plus one indicated on your RSVP card and would like to invite a date please reach out to us as soon as you are able to so that we can try to find available seating.
