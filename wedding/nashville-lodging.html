Transportation & Neighborhoods

Transportation

Columbia, TN is a little less than an hour’s ride south of Nashville. With that said, it may be the perfect place to stay if you are looking to stay longer than the weekend for a fuller vacation as there are plenty of fun activities, venues, shops, and restaurants to explore.

Unfortunately there is no public transportation between Columbia, TN and Nashville, TN. There are taxi services and Ubers that can be reserved in advanced. There are also car rental services that can be reserved at the airport. If you need assistance with transportation to and from the wedding please let us know in the RSVP form so that we can accommodate.
