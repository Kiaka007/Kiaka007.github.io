Transportation

Columbia, TN is a little less than an hours ride south of Nashville. It is a small, beautiful, picturesque town and is Wills childhood home. Unfortunately, there is no public transportation between Columbia, TN and Nashville, TN. There are taxi services and Ubers that can be reserved in advanced. There are also car rental services that can be reserved at the airport. If you need assistance with transportation to and from the wedding please let us know in the RSVP form so that we can accommodate.
